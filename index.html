3 activities for you!

Depending on your goals and abilities, at CrossFit Doko we offer several activities.

CrossFit

CrossFit is a functional, constantly varied, high-intensity training method.

CrossFit is suitable for everyone, from 6 to 90 years of age, and is a complete sport. You'll gain in strength, power, endurance, cardiovascular endurance, agility, balance, precision, flexibility and mental toughness.

Pilates

Pilates is a gentle form of gymnastics designed to strengthen and stretch muscles in depth.

It's a muscle-strengthening method based on precisely executed exercises that strengthen your core (transverse, back muscles, perineum), as well as your entire body.

Pilates is suitable for EVERYONE.

CrossFit

TRX (Total Resistance eXercise) is a form of bodybuilding that uses the body's own weight as resistance to the exercise being performed.

The main goal is to simultaneously develop strength, balance, flexibility and joint stability. The activity is suitable for everyone, as it can be adapted to the athlete's starting position.
